About Us

We are The Bahamars. The Bahamars is a group consisting of seven students from the University of Ghana

The Bay, was developed to aid learners acquire a skillset that would benefit them in more ways than one. This project was developed in conjunction with the Rehoboth Multimedia Center (RMC). The RMC is an institution that has well equipped facilities, trained personnel with adequate experience and a good learning structure to aid all who are interested in the app.
